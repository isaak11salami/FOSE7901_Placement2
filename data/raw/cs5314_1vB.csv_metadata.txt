This dataset contains the survival data of the first pathogenicity assay (Trial1) along with a subset of data from the second pathogenicity assay. This dataset was prepared to compare the survival data of Trial1 against the StockB data from the second pathogenicity assay.

Details pertaining to "subject_ID", "time" and "status" are described in the accompanying document, "raw_medata".

For details of treatment group Trial1, see "cs5314_dose assay_metadata". For details of the StockB treatment groups, see "cs5314_stock assay_metadata".
